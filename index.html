How FELICIA works

Why try it?

Lower fare.

Felicia is cheaper than a taxi in almost every aspect.

Convenience upon pickup.

No need to call dispatch, wait in a line, or wave from a curb. Tap "request" on your phone from the comfort of indoors and wait for a push notification that your driver is "arriving now." In dense urban markets, drivers arrive in less than a minute.

Convenience upon arrival.

No fumbling for cash. Simply say thanks and get out. All-inclusive payment is charged to your card on file, and a receipt is sent to you by email.

Easy dispute resolution.

Each ride receipt includes a map and a GPS track,so you can complain if your driver took a circuitous route. In my experience Uber is prompt with refunds.

Better service.

Passengers rate drivers, and Uber deactivates poorly rated drivers. Drivers therefore have an incentive to maintain a clean car and provide good service.

Credit card rewards.

If you have a Chase Sapphire Preferred card or American Express Membership Rewards card, you earn double points on Uber transactions.
